# Lendlee Landing Page - AI Build Prompt

## Project Overview

Build a marketing landing page for **Lendlee** - a community-powered lending and sharing app.

**Tagline:** "Lend freely. Care deeply. Stay connected."

**Core Value:** Generosity should not cost friendships.

---

## Design System

### Colors
- **Primary:** Sage green `#6B8F71`
- **Accent:** Terracotta `#C49A87`
- **Background:** Cream `#F5F2EB`
- **Text:** Earth `#403633`
- **Muted:** `#6B5D5A`

### Typography
- Headlines: Serif font (friendly, warm)
- Body: Clean sans-serif
- Tone: Warm, calm, community-first, non-corporate

### Feel
- No corporate/stiff language
- Gentle, kind, inviting
- Human-centered
- Avoid em dashes in copy

## Page Sections (in order)

### 1. Hero Section

Use the updated headline/subheadline above. This is the primary hero content.

---

### 2. Problem Section - The awkward moments

Four scenarios:
- 😅 **The awkward ask** - "Hey, do you still have my..."
- 😔 **The quiet resentment** - You miss the item. You do not want to seem petty.
- 🤐 **The friendship fade** - It was not a big deal. Now things feel different.
- 😕 **The accidental keeping** - Sometimes we forget we have something that belongs to someone else.

**Conclusion:** "Generosity should not cost friendships. There is a gentler way."

---

### 3. The Lendlee Way

**Headline:** "Remember gently. Return joyfully."

Four key points:
- **Gentle reminders** - "Quiet nudges at the right time for you, not them."
- **Mutual accountability** - "Both parties see the same simple truth."
- **No awkward conversations** - "The app remembers so you do not have to ask."
- **Relationship-first design** - "Built to protect trust, not transactions."

---

### 4. How It Works - Four Simple Steps

1. **Add an item** - "Snap a photo or type a title. Whatever you are sharing."
2. **Choose a person** - "Pick who is borrowing or getting it for free."
3. **Set a reminder** - "Choose when you would like a quiet nudge."
4. **Return and close the loop** - "When it comes back, mark it returned. Simple. No fuss."

*Here you can include a quick line like "Like Venmo for kindness" as a simple comparison.*

---

### 5. Three Ways to Share

**Headline:** "Share any way you want."

Show users can Give, Lend, or Promote:

| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **GIVE** | "Free. No strings attached. Give joyfully." |
| **LEND** | "Borrow and return. Your stuff comes back." |
| **PROMOTE** | "Share resources, events, knowledge with your community." |

**Share feature:** Each item has a share button that opens the phone native share sheet (Messages, WhatsApp) with pre-filled text.

## Key Copy Notes

- Avoid em dashes. Use shorter sentences or commas instead.
- Keep tone warm, not corporate.
- Emphasize: Give, Lend, Promote.
- Share feature is a key differentiator.
- Position as a movement, not just an app.
- Include alternative positioning lines naturally throughout.
