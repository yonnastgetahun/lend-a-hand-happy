# Lendlee Landing Page - AI Build Prompt (v3 - Resilience Edition)

## Project Overview

Build a marketing landing page for **Lendlee** - the community sharing app that builds neighborhood resilience.

**Core Concept:** Every share, lend, and gift builds a network of trust. The more your community shares, the stronger your emergency-ready network becomes.

**Tagline:** "Lend freely. Care deeply. Stay connected."

---

## Why This Matters Now

With growing concerns about economic uncertainty, climate events, and infrastructure disruption, communities need tools to share resources and communicate when it matters most. Lendlee makes neighborhood resilience feel accessible, not paranoid.

## Page Sections

### 1. The Problem (Why This Matters Now)

Today's world brings uncertainty:
- Economic cycles
- Climate events  
- Infrastructure disruption
- Community disconnection

**The old way:** We used to rely on neighbors. Now we barely know them.

**The answer:** Tools that make sharing natural and rebuild local trust.

---

### 2. Three Ways to Share

**Headline:** "Share Any Way You Want"

| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **GIVE** | "Free. No strings attached. Give joyfully." |
| **LEND** | "Borrow and return. Your stuff comes back." |
| **PROMOTE** | "Share resources, events, knowledge with your community." |

---

### 3. The Network Effect

**Headline:** "Every Share Builds Something Bigger"

> "The more your community shares, the more connected you become. Each transaction creates a node in your neighborhood's trust network. When infrastructure fails, these connections become your lifeline."

- More sharing = stronger community bonds
- More interactions = more people you can reach offline
- More trust = faster coordination in emergencies

---

### 4. How It Works - Four Simple Steps

1. **Add an item** - "Snap a photo or type a title. Whatever you are sharing."
2. **Choose a person** - "Pick who is borrowing or getting it for free."
3. **Set a reminder** - "Choose when you would like a quiet nudge."
4. **Return and close the loop** - "When it comes back, mark it returned."

---

### 5. The Technology Behind It

**Headline:** "Built for the Unexpected"

Lendlee uses decentralized technology so your community stays connected when it matters most:

- **Offline-First** - "Bluetooth mesh networking allows communication even when internet is down."
- **No Phone Numbers** - "Nostr-based identity means no personal data, ever."
- **Decentralized** - "No central servers means no single point of failure."
- **Privacy First** - "Your data stays yours. Always."

*Simple explanation: "Like having a neighborhood radio that works when everything else fails."*

---

### 6. Emergency Ready

**Headline:** "When It Counts"
