# Lendlee - App Store & Play Store Listing Copy

---

## App Store (iOS)

### App Name (30 char max)
Lendlee

### Subtitle (30 char max)
Track What You Lend to Friends

### Promotional Text (170 char max)
Lending makes the heart grow fonder. Track what you share with friends and neighbors, send friendly SMS reminders when it's time to return, and keep relationships whole.

### Description (4000 char max)

We've all been there. You lend a book to a friend, a set of tools to your neighbor, or a casserole dish to a family member -- and weeks later, you can't remember who has what.

Lendlee is the simple, thoughtful way to keep track of everything you share with the people around you. No more awkward "do you still have my...?" conversations. No more lost items. Just generosity that feels good, from start to finish.

Lend freely. Remember kindly.

HOW LENDLEE WORKS

Add an item you're lending -- a book, a kitchen gadget, camping gear, power tools, or anything else. Pick the person from your contacts, set an optional return date, and you're done. Lendlee remembers so you don't have to.

When the return date is approaching, send a friendly SMS reminder right from the app. It's casual, not confrontational -- just a gentle nudge that keeps things on track and relationships intact.

FEATURES

- Quick item entry: Add what you're lending in seconds. Name it, snap a photo, and pick a category.
- Contact picker: Choose who you're lending to directly from your phone contacts. No manual entry needed.
- Return timeframes: Set expected return dates so nothing slips through the cracks.
- SMS reminders: Send a friendly text reminder when an item is overdue, right from the app.
- Lending history: See a complete record of everything you've lent and to whom -- past and present.
- Smart categories: Organize items by type -- books, tools, kitchen, electronics, games, and more.
- Apple and Google Sign-In: Get started in seconds with the account you already have.
- Privacy-first design: Your contact list is never uploaded. Lendlee only accesses contacts when you choose someone to lend to.

WHO LENDLEE IS FOR

- Book lovers who share their collection with friends
- Neighbors who swap tools, ladders, and yard equipment
- Parents lending board games, baby gear, and sports equipment
- Home cooks sharing specialty pans, appliances, and serving dishes
- Anyone who is generous but forgetful

YOUR STUFF, YOUR MEMORY

Lendlee isn't about being possessive or keeping score. It's about being generous without the mental load. When you know you'll remember, you lend more freely. When you lend more freely, your community grows stronger.

Start tracking what you share. Download Lendlee today.
